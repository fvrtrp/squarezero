There's a spark, there's a flame.
There's a dream, there's reality.
There's what once was, there's what's now, there's what could be.
There's happiness, there's joy springing from the heart.
There's disappointment, there's a reunion. 
There's ego, there's vengeance, there's sympathy. 
There's hope, there's betrayal, there's convergence. 
There are qualms, there are doubts, there are moments of purity. 
There's a delusion, there's expectation, there's acceptance. 
There's hope, there's an instinct, there's destiny.
